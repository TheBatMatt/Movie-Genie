The Movie Genie

Undefined - the plot. An unfortunate accident sets in motion a path of change for Nathanial Cane as he attempts to recover from substance abuse. A mentally unstable psychiatrist, DR. Henry Calhoon is tipping over the edge into a series of guilt driven hallucinations. In the background a powerful figure is manipulating them both. Intertwined in this struggle for control is a well-meaning vigilante, who falls into the mystery of the perception of good and evil. As the threads untwine the distinctions between characters and their relationships breakdown to reveal a simple human need shared by all.
